What?

The first week at Quantum Weave was primarily an introduction and onboarding process to understand the WIL Project and the company. I participated in activities to get to know the team, understand the culture, and start building a communication plan. I also learned about the "LiveMesh" system, an innovative technology for task distribution between devices and the cloud, which is the core of the company’s product.

So What?

This week made me realize the importance of quickly adapting to a real professional environment beyond technical skills, focusing also on soft skills like communication and teamwork. Understanding Quantum Weave’s context and vision motivated me to appreciate interdisciplinarity and innovation, which are crucial for a full stack developer collaborating with different specialists.

Now What?

For next week, I will focus on applying what I learned about effective communication to coordinate with my team. I will work on establishing a clear plan to share information, avoid misunderstandings, and foster collaboration. I will also prepare my technical skills to confidently tackle project tasks, maintaining an open attitude toward learning and feedback.
